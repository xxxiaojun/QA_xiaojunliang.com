    logo concept

    The design of the logo is based on the golden ratio, with references to deconstructivism and the Bauhaus for inspiration. Part of the golden ratio is selected, and a combination of curves and straight lines is used. The founder's philosophy interior design is not decoration, but through the reasonable organization and design of the space.

    Color Palettes

    The color scheme chooses the three primary colors in color theory. Visually, their contrast is reduced to make them harmonious. Conceptually, the three primary colors are the foundation of all colors and are the beginning. From a cultural point of view, it symbolizes the beginning of the exploration of design, which is integrated with the concept of the brand.

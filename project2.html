    About Kitty Dumpling

    kitty is a Japanese dumpling shop established in 2021. Run by two young cat lovers and cat shop manager Miku. Adhere to the combination of handmade and healthy organic ingredients to create a modern healthy taste.

    Color Palettes

    The color scheme is based on the color of the product and the trend of color matching, while orange can stimulate appetite, green represents nature, and the contrast between colors is reduced to make the vision more harmonious and more intimate.

    About logo

    In the early stages of logo conception, a rough thumbnail of the logo was sketched, repeated iterations, and digital production using Adobe Illustrator to refine the details and color scheme of the logo.

    Poster & Menu

    The poster design uses the combination of food pictures and illustrations, in order to let the audience understand the products sold by the store more intuitively.

    The menu design, hopefully, is simple and easy to understand, and better reflects the selling point. However, it also helps to improve the efficiency of ordering. Customers only need to order simple steps to get customized flavors.

    Product packaging

    Packaging design is often used to reflect of branding, using the form of illustrations for easy identification.

    gift card

    Use marketing strategies to design special styles for festivals to attract and cater to the different needs of customers.

    Interior design

    The visual of the storefront is also conceived using branding, which can help the design concept and vision to be better expressed and communicated to the audience. This provides a more detailed reference for future project development.
